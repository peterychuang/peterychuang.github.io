Peter Y. Chuang is a Hong Kong-born novelist who has been educated in London and calls Berlin his spiritual home. He has completed the manuscript of a literary science fiction novel, "Twenty Forty-Seven," and is currently re-writing another literary novel, "Only You Know What It Means."
